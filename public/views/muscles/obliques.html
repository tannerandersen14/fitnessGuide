OBLIQUES

Side Plank

  • Lie on your side and support your body between your forearm and knee to your feet.
  • Hold the movement, keeping your core tight

'

Dumbbell Side Bend

  • While keeping your back straight and your head up, bend only at the waist to the right as far as possible.
  • Breathe in as you bend to the side. Then hold for a second and come back up to the starting position as you exhale.

Alternating Heel Touches

  • Crunch over your torso forward and up about 3-4 inches to the right side and touch your right heel as you hold the contraction for a second.
  • Now go back slowly to the starting position as you inhale.
  • Now crunch over your torso forward and up around 3-4 inches to the left side and touch your left heel as you hold the contraction for a second.
  • Continue alternating sides in this manner until all prescribed repetitions are done.

Cross-Body Crunch

  • Curl up and bring your right elbow and shoulder across your body while bring your left knee in toward your left shoulder at the same time.
  • Reach with your elbow and try to touch your knee.
  • Now go back down to the starting position as you inhale and repeat with the left elbow and the right knee.
